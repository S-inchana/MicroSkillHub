About Us

Vision & Mission

Our long-term vision is to create a self-sustaining network of micro-hubs that catalyze economic independence across rural India. Our mission is delivering accessible, high-quality, and contextually relevant skill training.

Core Focus

We are dedicated to uplifting women, persons with disabilities (PWD), and underserved youth within rural communities, ensuring equitable access to market-ready skills and job creation opportunities.

Origin Story

Born from a recognized need to bridge the technology and skill gap, the Micro Skill Hub concept was prototyped to bring decentralized, modular, and affordable learning centers directly to the villages.
